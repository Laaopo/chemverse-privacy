Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

ChemVerse respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard information in compliance with Google Play Store, COPPA, and GDPR regulations.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and email (only if account is created)
  • Learning progress and app usage data
  • Camera access for AR features (no storage)
  • Anonymous AI tutor text inputs

2. How We Use Data

  • Personalized learning experience
  • App performance and feature improvement
  • Security and misuse prevention

3. Data Sharing

ChemVerse does not sell or share personal data. Limited anonymous analytics may be used for app improvement.

4. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary. Users may request deletion at any time.

5. Children’s Privacy

ChemVerse is designed for Classes 6–12. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

6. Your Rights

  • Access or update your data
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
