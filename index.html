Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

ChemVerse respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard information in compliance with Google Play Store, COPPA, and GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name and email (only if you create an account).
  • Usage Data: App interactions, quiz progress, and learning analytics.
  • Camera Access: Used exclusively for AR learning features. No images or videos are stored or shared.
  • AI Tutor Queries: Anonymous text inputs to improve learning accuracy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize learning paths and content.
  • To improve app performance and educational quality.
  • To ensure platform safety and prevent misuse.

3. Data Sharing

ChemVerse does not sell, rent, or trade user data. Limited data may be processed by trusted third-party services (analytics, crash reporting) strictly for app improvement and security.

4. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide app functionality or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time.

5. Children's Privacy

ChemVerse is designed for students (Classes 6–12). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

6. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request account deletion

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
